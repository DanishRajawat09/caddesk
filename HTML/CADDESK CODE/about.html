Our Story

CADDESK Centre was established in 2005 with a vision to provide industry-relevant technical education in Computer-Aided Design (CAD) and Information Technology. What started as a small training center with a handful of students has now grown into one of the leading technical education institutes with multiple centers across the country.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, adaptation to industry trends, and an unwavering commitment to student success. We have trained over 40,000 students who have gone on to build successful careers in various technical fields.

Today, CADDESK Centre stands as a symbol of quality education, practical training, and career-focused learning in the field of CAD and IT education.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower students with practical skills and knowledge in CAD and IT domains through quality education, hands-on training, and industry exposure, enabling them to achieve professional excellence.

Our Vision

To be the premier institute for technical education, recognized for excellence in teaching, innovation, and producing industry-ready professionals who contribute significantly to the technological advancement of society.

What Sets Us Apart

Expert Faculty

Our instructors are industry professionals with years of practical experience, ensuring that you learn from the best in the field.

Hands-on Training

We believe in learning by doing. Our curriculum focuses on practical exercises and real-world projects to build your skills.

State-of-the-art Facilities

Our centers are equipped with modern computer labs, software, and infrastructure to provide an optimal learning environment.

Placement Assistance

We have a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to connect students with job opportunities through campus interviews and networking.
