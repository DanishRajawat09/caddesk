Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in our courses by visiting your nearest CADDESK center, calling our helpline, or filling out the inquiry form on our website. Our counselors will guide you through the enrollment process and help you choose the right course.

What are the payment options available?

We offer various payment options including full payment, installment plans, and education loans through our banking partners. We also offer scholarships for deserving students. Please contact our admission counselors for details.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive placement assistance to all our students. Our dedicated placement cell works with numerous industry partners to help students find suitable job opportunities. We also conduct regular placement drives and interview preparation sessions.

Are there any prerequisites for joining the courses?

Prerequisites vary depending on the course. While some basic courses don't require any prior knowledge, advanced courses may need foundational understanding. Our counselors will assess your background and recommend the most suitable course for you.

Do you offer online courses?

Yes, we offer online versions of most of our courses. Our online programs feature the same curriculum as in-center courses, with live instructor-led sessions, practical projects, and all learning materials available through our learning management system.
