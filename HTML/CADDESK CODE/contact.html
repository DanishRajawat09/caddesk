Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our admissions process, courses, and facilities

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in a course by visiting our nearest training center, filling out the contact form on this page, or by calling our admissions helpline. Our admissions team will guide you through the enrollment process including course selection, fee payment, and class scheduling.

What are the qualifications needed for admission?

Requirements vary by program. Most of our courses are open to students from all educational backgrounds with basic computer knowledge. For advanced courses, certain prerequisites may apply. Please contact our admissions team for specific course requirements.

Do you offer placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that works with industry partners to provide job opportunities to our students. We offer resume building, interview preparation, and campus recruitment drives to help our students find suitable employment.

Are there any scholarships available?

We offer merit-based scholarships to deserving students. Additionally, we provide special discounts for group enrollments and for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Please inquire at our admissions office for more details.

What is the duration of the courses?

Course durations vary depending on the program. Short certificate courses can range from 2-4 weeks, while comprehensive diploma programs may extend from 3-6 months. We also offer flexible timing options to accommodate working professionals and students.
