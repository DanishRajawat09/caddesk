Our Story

Founded in 2005, CAD DESK Institute has grown from a small training center to a premier educational institute specializing in CAD (Computer-Aided Design) and IT education. Over the years, we have trained thousands of students who are now successful professionals in their respective fields.

Our journey has been characterized by a commitment to excellence in technical education, continuously updating our curriculum to meet industry demands and investing in state-of-the-art training facilities to provide the best learning experience for our students.
