Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and admissions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by visiting our campus, filling out the contact form on this page, or calling our admissions office. After submitting your application, our team will guide you through the enrollment process including course selection and fee payment.

What are the qualifications needed for admission?

Requirements vary by program. Basic courses require a high school diploma or equivalent, while advanced programs may require some prior knowledge or a relevant degree. Please contact our admissions office for specific requirements for your program of interest.

Do you offer placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that helps students find job opportunities through campus interviews, job fairs, and industry connections. Our placement services include resume building, interview preparation, and career counseling.

Are there any scholarships available?

We offer merit-based scholarships and financial aid options for deserving students. Eligibility criteria vary for different programs. Please contact our financial aid office for more information about scholarship opportunities.
