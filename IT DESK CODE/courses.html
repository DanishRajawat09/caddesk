Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our courses and enrollment

What are the prerequisites for enrolling in these courses?

Most of our beginner courses don't require previous experience. For intermediate and advanced courses, basic knowledge of the subject or completion of prerequisite courses may be required. Specific requirements are listed on each course page.

Are there any installment payment options available?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for most of our courses. You can pay in 2-3 installments depending on the course duration and fees. Please contact our admissions office for detailed payment options.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes, all students receive a course completion certificate. For professional certification courses, you'll be prepared to take the official certification exam, which is typically included in the course fee.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes, we have a dedicated placement cell that helps students find job opportunities through campus interviews, job fairs, and industry connections. Our placement services include resume building, interview preparation, and career counseling.

What are the classroom schedules and timings?

We offer flexible batch timings to accommodate different schedules. Classes typically run on weekdays (morning, afternoon, and evening batches) and weekends. Each session is approximately 2-3 hours long. Please contact our institute for the latest schedule information for your chosen course.
